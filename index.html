About me

I design learning experiences and streamline processes to help organizations work smarter. My work sits at the intersection of creativity, clarity, and operational precision.

With experience across education and manufacturing, I build engaging e-learning solutions, manage LMS ecosystems, and craft multimedia content using Adobe Creative Suite, Articulate 360, and Microsoft 365. I’ve improved workflows, trained teams, and elevated user experiences by blending design thinking with analytical problem-solving. Adaptable, quick to learn, and clear in communication, I connect creative ideas with practical execution to deliver results that make a measurable difference.

Currently working full-time, but open to collaborations, freelance opportunities, or interesting design challenges.
